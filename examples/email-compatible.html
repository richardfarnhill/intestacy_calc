About This Version

This version of the calculator is designed to be compatible with email marketing campaigns and newsletters. Instead of embedding the interactive calculator directly, this example shows how to create a link to the calculator with a brief description.

When using in emails, you would typically:

  1. Create a landing page with the full calculator
  2. Include a brief description and compelling CTA in your email
  3. Link to the landing page for the interactive experience

What Happens to Your Estate Without a Will?

Did you know that if you die without a valid Will, your estate will be distributed according to intestacy rules?

These rules may not reflect your wishes, and could leave loved ones without inheritance - especially unmarried partners.
